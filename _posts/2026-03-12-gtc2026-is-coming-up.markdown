**\#GTC2026** kicks off next week! 🚀

One area I'm particularly excited to delve into is the latest from NVIDIA on **#GPU** **#accelerated** **#analytics**. It's been incredibly impressive to see GPU hardware performance improve from generation to generation. CPUs and GPUs are getting more tightly integrated in ever larger data center clusters, memory and communication bottlenecks are quickly diminishing, and NVIDIA's **#RAPIDS** **#AI** software stack underpinning distributed query engines is maturing. Indeed, it seems we are at the cusp of a new era of GPU-accelerated data exploration and productionization, one that will most likely be driven by the explosion of AI data agents in the enterprise.
